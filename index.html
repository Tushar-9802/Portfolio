Bawarchi - Role-Aware Recipe Generation

Multimodal AI system combining computer vision and NLP for cuisine-aware recipe generation. Three-model pipeline: YOLOv8m detection (66.51% mAP, 124 classes), PMI + embedding substitution (85% precision), and Llama 3.2 3B fine-tuning (74.1% accuracy on 335K recipes).

Pioneered role-aware reasoning for ingredient interchangeability across cuisines—same ingredients generate tacos (Mexican) or roti wrap (Indian) based on cultural context. Manually curated 1K role-based examples and quality-filtered 43K synthetic samples (54% acceptance). Published 68 MB training dataset with cuisine-specific PMI matrices and 202 manual Indian spice annotations from Mendeley.

PyTorch, Transformers, PEFT (LoRA), YOLOv8, Sentence-Transformers, Streamlit, CUDA
